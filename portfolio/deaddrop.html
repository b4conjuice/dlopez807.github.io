share info:

swap stash:

share secrets:
show your two cards to another player. if that player has a card equal to the sum of your two cards, they must swap that card with one of your cards of your choice
grab drop:
look at the drop. if it equals to the sum of your hand, you win. otherwise, you're out of the game.
